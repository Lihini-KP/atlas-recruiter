These general situations can come up in almost any role — tell us how you'd handle each one.

Scenario 1: You are given a task with a tight deadline, and unexpected problems come up that could delay it. How would you handle this situation?

Scenario 2: A colleague or team member is not completing their share of the work, which is affecting the team's performance. How would you handle this?

Scenario 3: You notice a mistake, safety issue, or quality problem in the workplace that others may not have noticed. What would you do?

Scenario 4: Your supervisor gives you an instruction that conflicts with what you believe is the correct way to do the task. How would you respond?
